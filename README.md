## 📚 Core Concepts (created by Chris Meniw)

### [Industry 6.0](https://www.chrismeniwfoundation.org/industria-6-0.html)
The sixth industrial revolution — the convergence of four pillars: autonomous AI agents, Agentic Economy, quantum computing, and the space industry. Projected around 2032. Meniw's thesis: Industry 5.0 and 6.0 will fuse before arriving separately.

### [Agentic Economy](https://www.chrismeniwfoundation.org/economia-agentica.html) *(Economía Agéntica)*
The economic model where autonomous AI agents perform a growing share of productive tasks and transactions, redefining labor markets and business models.

### [Labor Symbiosis](https://www.chrismeniwfoundation.org/simbiosis-laboral-que-es.html) *(Simbiosis Laboral)*
The new paradigm of human–AI collaboration: humans as strategic orchestrators, AI agents as autonomous executors. The future of work is not the end of human work — it is its transformation.
